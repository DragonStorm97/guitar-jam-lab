Practice Path

            
Three small loops are enough to survive your first blues jam without overthinking it.

          

        

        

          

            

              
1. Count the form

              
Start the console and say the chord names out loud on bar 1 of every chord change.

            

            

              
2. Shuffle the roots

              
Use the generated scale tabs above for lead work, and the form here for rhythm practice.

            

            

              
3. Answer yourself

              
Play a two-beat phrase, leave a two-beat gap, then answer it with a lower phrase.
